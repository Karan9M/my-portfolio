## What is OpenClaw, Actually?
OpenClaw, affectionately dubbed the "lobster" by its community, has its roots in "Clawdbot" and "Moltbot", which were originally built by Austrian developer Peter Steinberger. Fast forward to today, and it's evolved into what many are calling the "Linux of Agents". But what does that really mean? In simple terms, OpenClaw is an open-source platform that allows you to automate tasks, integrate with various services, and even build custom agents to make your life easier.
