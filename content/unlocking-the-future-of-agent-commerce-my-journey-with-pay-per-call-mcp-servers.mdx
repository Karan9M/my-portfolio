## The Power of MCP Servers
So, what's an MCP server, you ask? In simple terms, it's a local server that gives AI agents access to crypto intelligence, such as preflight checks, trade signals, and risk state, all for a small payment in USDC on Base. The project that caught my attention is called coinopai-mcp, and it's available on GitHub. What I found fascinating was the payment layer and how it's teaching us about the future of agent commerce.
