# The Open Source Journey: From Hidden Gems to AI Powerhouses
 *_Unlocking the secrets of the open source world and its impact on the future of software_* 

Hey there! I'm Karan, and today I want to talk about something that I think is really exciting - the open source community and its connection to the rapidly evolving world of AI. 🤔

## The Unsung Heroes of Open Source
When we think about open source projects, we often remember the big ones like react-boilerplate and styled-components. However, there are hundreds of other projects that nobody remembers, but they have played a significant role in shaping the tech landscape. I recently listened to an interview with Max Stoiber, who is currently working on ChatGPT's plugin directory and app platform at OpenAI, and it got me thinking about the importance of these hidden gems.

### The Story of Spectrum and GitHub Discussions
One of the projects that Max worked on was Spectrum, a community platform that eventually became part of GitHub and helped shape GitHub Discussions. This is a great example of how open source projects can have a significant impact on the tech industry. The fact that Spectrum was able to contribute to the development of GitHub Discussions shows that even smaller open source projects can make a big difference.

### The Power of Founder Growth
Max also talked about the founder growth that came from building Stellate, a GraphQL cache that turned into a dual acquisition by Shopify and The Guild. This story highlights the potential for open source projects to lead to significant founder growth and even acquisitions. It's a testament to the fact that the open source community is a great place to build and showcase your skills, and it can lead to amazing opportunities.

### ChatGPT Apps: A New Surface for Software
The conversation with Max also touched on the topic of ChatGPT apps and how they feel like a new surface for software. I think this is a really interesting point, and it got me thinking about the potential for AI to change the way we interact with software. With ChatGPT apps, we're seeing a new level of complexity and capability that is opening up new possibilities for developers and users alike.

## My Take
Personally, I think the connection between open source and AI is really exciting. I believe that the open source community has the potential to play a significant role in shaping the future of AI, and it's going to be interesting to see how this evolves. The fact that companies like OpenAI are investing in open source projects and communities is a great sign, and it shows that they recognize the value of collaboration and community-driven development.

## Conclusion
In conclusion, the open source community is a powerful force that has the potential to shape the future of software and AI. From hidden gems to AI powerhouses, the open source world is full of exciting projects and opportunities. Whether you're a seasoned developer or just starting out, I think it's worth exploring the open source community and seeing what you can learn and contribute.
