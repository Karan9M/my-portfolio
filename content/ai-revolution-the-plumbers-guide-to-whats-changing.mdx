## The Big News
The Model Context Protocol (MCP) has just locked its largest revision ever, and it's going stateless. But what does that even mean? In simple terms, MCP is a set of rules that helps AI models talk to each other and to the tools that use them. Going stateless means that these models can now work more efficiently and scale better. 🚀

### How This Affects You
1. **Faster AI model training** — with the new MCP, training AI models just got a whole lot faster.
2. **More efficient models** — stateless models are lighter and more efficient, which means they can run on smaller devices.
3. **Better collaboration** — with standardized protocols, different AI systems can work together seamlessly.
