## What is Proof?
Proof is a decentralized learning management system built on EduChain, a blockchain-based platform that enables secure, transparent, and tamper-proof storage of academic records. The project started taking shape on July 28, 2025, with the goal of solving the problem of unreliable or easily altered academic records. By putting key learning actions like course enrollment, exam creation, submissions, and scores on-chain, Proof ensures that academic records are immutable and verifiable.

## How it Works
The idea behind Proof is simple yet powerful. By leveraging the security and transparency of blockchain technology, the platform provides a secure and reliable way to store and manage academic records. This means that students, tutors, and educational institutions can trust that the records are accurate and tamper-proof. The platform also enables real-time tracking of student progress, making it easier for tutors to identify areas where students need improvement.

## Demo and Features
The Proof project has come a long way since its inception, and the demo showcases the platform's capabilities.
