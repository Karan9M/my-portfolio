2. **Innovation stagnation** — By limiting access to their best model, Anthropic may be stifling innovation and progress in the AI community.
3. **Exclusive opportunities** — The 50 companies that are part of the consortium will have a significant advantage over others, which could lead to an uneven playing field.

The Anthropic news is just one example of a larger trend in the AI industry. As AI models become more powerful and sophisticated, we're seeing a shift towards more exclusive and controlled access.
