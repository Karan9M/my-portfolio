## What's the Big Deal About Kimi K3?
So, what makes Kimi K3 so special? For starters, it's a mixture-of-experts (MoE) model with a massive 1-million-token context window, native vision capabilities, and a brand new architecture built on Kimi Delta Attention (KDA) and Attention Residuals (AttnRes). This means that K3 is capable of handling complex tasks with ease, making it a direct challenger to other AI models like Claude Fable 5 and GPT-5.6 Sol.
