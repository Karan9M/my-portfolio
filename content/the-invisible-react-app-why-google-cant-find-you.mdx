## What are Core Web Vitals, Anyway?
Core Web Vitals are a set of metrics that measure the user experience of your app. They include things like Largest Contentful Paint (LCP), Cumulative Layout Shift (CLS), and First Input Delay (FID). These metrics are used by Google to determine the search ranking of your app. But here's the thing - just because your app feels fast to you, it doesn't mean it's optimized for search engines.
